Spy

    Class Data
    Spy
    Wide Class Rogue
    Skill Points 4
    Specials Shadowy Connections, Stealth Expert
    Languages Thieves' Cant
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 DEX, +1 AGI, +1 CHA

    While thieves steal material wealth and try to get rich, spies steal information, using it for their own use or selling it to others. A skilled spy can remain undetected for decades, passing valuable knowledge to their sponsors in high places, and ready to vanish at a moment's notice. Spies can be stealthy when necessary, but also know when to hide in plain sight instead. An experience spy trusts in nothing, but remains patient in the hopes that some important tidbit of information will come their way if they don't upset the situation â€” along with a generous bonus from their employer, of course.

    Shadowy Connections

    Spies, like all Rogues, always have connections with the criminal underworld. As such, other people of low repute tend to trust them, and will give them information or even aid in exchange for enough gold.

    Stealth Expert

    Spies are used to sneaking around, and receive a double agility bonus to stealth.

    Thieves' Cant

    Spies learn how to speak Thieves' Cant, and can often write it phonetically using the Common or Elvish character set.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Rogues gain a +1 bonus to dexterity and charisma.

    Spies gain an additional +1 bonus to agility.

    Skills

    Rogues gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge, Mental, Mental: Deception, Mental: Performance, Physical: Acrobatics, Weapons, and Weapons: Piercing.

    Spies gain an additional level in Mental: Deception, Mental: Investigation, Mental: Perception, Physical: Endurance, and Weapons: Improvised.

    Overall, spies have 1 level in knowledge skills, 6 levels in mental skills, 2 levels in physical skills, and 3 levels in weapon skills.

    Spies also receive an additional 4 skill points to spend.
