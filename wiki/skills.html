Skills

    Skills are a core aspect of Amathereon. They allow you to be better at specific areas, providing more precision than ability scores. Skills are divided into four sections: Knowledge, Mental, Physical and Weapons. Knowledge skills let you know more about a specific kind of thing: if you want to get information about a tool used in magic, you will be more likely to recall the information quickly if you have a high level in Knowledge: Arcana. Mental skills improve how you interact with others and the world around you: what you can notice, think of doing, and make people feel. For example, Mental: Deception makes you a better liar. Physical skills let you use your body better: you are less likely to trip if you have a high level for Physical: Acrobatics. Finally, Weapons skills give you bonuses to using weapons of specific types. If you're using a bow, you'll get bonuses out of the Weapons skill, as well as Weapons: Ranged and Weapons: Piercing.

    Skill Listing

    Knowledge

    The Knowledge skill provides a 10% bonus to every use of a skill in the Knowledge category, and boosts the effectiveness of each skill in the category by 10%.

    Knowledge: Arcana

    Knowledge: Arcana is used to provide a bonus to detecting magic, recalling facts about magic, and detecting magic in a mundane fashion.

    Knowledge: Culture

    Knowledge: Culture is used to provide a bonus to recalling facts about cultures all across Amathereon, to boost fashion sense, and to deal with religious matters.

    Knowledge: History

    Knowledge: History is used to provide a bonus to remembering historical events and other facts about the past.

    Knowledge: Linguistics

    Knowledge: Linguistics is used to help detect what language someone is speaking or writing in, and to understand foreign languages.

    Knowledge: Medicine

    Knowledge: Medicine is used to understand health, anatomy, and chemicals, and to provide a bonus in terms of basic first aid.

    Knowledge: Wilderness

    Knowledge: Wilderness is used to provide a bonus to survival skills across the board: recognizing flora and fauna, finding water, lighting fires, and tracking.

    Mental

    The Mental skill provides a 10% bonus to every use of a skill in the Mental category, and boosts the effectiveness of each skill in the category by 10%.

    Mental: Deception

    Mental: Deception is used to convince people that you're telling the truth, even if you're not. If you have a low score in Mental: Deception, people with high Mental: Perception scores might receive hints that something's up.

    Mental: Intimdation

    Mental: Intimidation is used to invoke fear in people using your charisma, to convince them to do what you say.

    Mental: Investigation

    Mental: Investigation is used to help solve puzzles and to figure out why something is the way it is.

    Mental: Perception

    Mental: Perception is used to search for items, to detect oddities about people or situations, to reveal hidden characters or objects, and for tracking.

    Mental: Performance

    Mental: Performance is used to put on a show for people, whether it be to distract them or simply to tell a story.

    Mental: Persuasion

    Mental: Persuasion is used to convince NPCs that your perspective is valid and to sway them to your point of view.

    Physical

    The Physical skill provides a 10% bonus to every use of a skill in the Physical category, and boosts the effectiveness of each skill in the category by 10%.

    Physical: Acrobatics

    Physical: Acrobatics is used to pull stunts, scramble up steep slopes, and jump long distances.

    Physical: Athletics

    Physical: Athletics is used to lift weights, increase your carrying limit, arm wrestle, and grapple with your enemies.

    Physical: Endurance

    Physical: Endurance is used to increase the length of time you can last without needing a break. Each level of Physical: Endurance you have increases your maximum energy by 10 points.

    Physical: Intimdation

    Physical: Intimidation is used to intimidate people by threatening them directly. It is more effective than Mental: Intimdation for brawnier characters who are less charismatic.

    Physical: Sleight of Hand

    Physical: Sleight of Hand is used to handle objects without others noticing, whether it be to steal something or to pull a coin from behind someone's ear.

    Physical: Stealth

    Physical: Stealth is used to hide from characters, and can also be used to move without setting off alarms or having anyone notice you.

    Weapons

    Each level of the Weapons skill provides a +1 hit bonus and +1 damage bonus to every attack you make.

    Weapons: Improvised is used to improve your skill with unarmed combat and improvised weapons. With each level of this skill, you gain a +5 hit bonus and +5 parry bonus when wielding makeshift weapons â€” or nothing.

    Weapons: Melee

    Weapons: Melee is used to improve your skill with melee weapons. With each level of this skill, you gain a +5 hit bonus and +5 parry bonus when wielding a proper melee weapon.

    Weapons: Piercing

    Weapons: Piercing is used to improve your skill with piercing weapons. With each level of this skill, you gain a +5 hit bonus and +1 damage bonus for attacks with weapons that deal piercing damage.

    Weapons: Ranged

    Weapons: Ranged is used to improve your skill with ranged weapons. With each level of this skill, you gain a +10 hit bonus for attacks made with ranged weapons.

    Weapons: Slashing

    Weapons: Slashing is used to improve your skill with slashing weapons. With each level of this skill, you gain a +5 hit bonus and +1 damage bonus for attacks with weapons that deal slashing damage.

    Weapons: Trauma

    Weapons: Trauma is used to improve your skill with hammers, maces, and cleaving weapons. With each level of this skill, you gain a +5 hit bonus and +1 damage bonus for attacks with weapons that deal trauma damage.
