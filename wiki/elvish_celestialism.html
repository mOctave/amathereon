    Elvish Celestialism is a particular branch of Celestial worship, where believers pray continually for the Celestials to intercede on their behalf, honouring them all, but especially Alaforonaist'laiezeron, the creator. Because of the historical roots of this religion, many of its believers are exceptionally confident in their faith, and reject the validity of all other religions.

    Elvish Celestialism is not an organized religion: there is neither clergy nor designated places of worship. The few religious texts that exist are used elsewhere as historical documents, and Laraidor, which followers claim as their historical centre, is not to be found anywhere on Amathereon.
