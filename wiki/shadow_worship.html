    Shadow Worship, the official religion of Uruthl, is the acknowledgement of Arakh as the supreme diety above all others. The Dread Lord encourages Shadow Worship whenever possible, but accepts other religions, seeking to convert by choice rather than force and, in so doing, bring people to wholly devote themselves to Chaos. Shadow Worship is very rare outside of Uruthl, and even inside of Uruthl, it is in many places usurped by the Cult of the Old Spirits and Grireverchism as the primary religion.

    In the past twenty years, the number of human followers of Shadow Worship has nearly doubled, with the expansion of the Fifth Department of the Dread Land and the threat of Arakh's armies being palpable in many places.
