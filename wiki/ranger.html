Ranger

    Class Data
    Ranger
    Wide Class Outlander
    Skill Points 4
    Specials Wildcraft, Tracking
    Languages Choose One
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 DEX, +1 AGI, +1 CON

    Rangers are wanderers of the sort that tend to make it into ballads unwillingly. A ranger is generally calm and confident, but not particularly social. Rangers guard against Chaos where soldiers never go, but they are still feared, for few ever see them but everyone knows of their existence.

    Most rangers are uncomfortable being in the spotlight. They seek to accomplish their goals — normally preventing Chaos from destroying civilization — while avoiding being acclaimed for it. Rangers chose to spend their lives away from other people for a reason, and they rarely willingly return. A ranger who turns to Chaos will never be a lord, but they might be a spymaster or military commader instead.

    Wildcraft

    Rangers receive a 50% bonus to any use of the Knowledge: Wilderness skill. Additionally, rangers can choose a non-urban environment in which to gain a 20% bonus to tracking and stealth.

    Tracking

    Rangers receive a 100% bonus to any tracking attempts.

    Select a Language

    Rangers can choose one additional language in which they can both speak, read, and write. This language cannot be Thieves' Cant or Druidic.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Outlanders gain a +1 bonus to agility and constitution.

    Rangers gain an additional +1 bonus to dexterity.

    Skills

    Outlanders gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge: Medicine, Knowledge: Wilderness, Mental: Investigation, Mental: Perception, Physical, Physical: Endurance, and Weapons: Ranged.

    Rangers gain an additional level in Knowledge: Wilderness, Mental: Perception, Physical: Endurance, Physical: Stealth, and Weapons: Ranged.

    Overall, rangers have 3 levels in knowledge skills, 3 levels in mental skills, 4 levels in physical skills, and 2 levels in weapon skills.

    Rangers also receive an additional 4 skill points to spend.
