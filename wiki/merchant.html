Merchant

    Class Data
    Merchant
    Wide Class Specialist
    Skill Points 6
    Specials Haggling
    Languages None
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 CON, +1 WIS, +1 CHA

    There are merchants almost everywhere, and they tend to be much the same wherever you go. A merchant cares first and foremost for their profits, but secondly for their reputation, and only third for their personal life. A skilled merchant can sell you the air you breathe or the coat you wear without it seeming out of the ordinary to you.

    Although merchants tend to all be focused on making the gold in their pockets jingle the loudest — typically by increasing its volume — how they go about it depends on the person. Some prioritize their reputation for quality, selling only the best and charging a premium. Others only care about how much they can sell: their quality tends to be very poor, but their prices are also cheaper and they rarely choose to haggle over them.

    Haggling

    Merchants receive 5% off all purchases, and +5% to all profits from sales.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Merchants gain a +1 bonus to constitution, wisdom, and charisma.

    Skills

    Specialists gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge, Mental, Physical, and Weapons.

    Merchants gain an additional level in Knowledge, Knowledge: Culture, Mental: Deceptiobn, Mental: Perception, Mental: Performance, Mental: Persuasion, Physical: Endurance and Weapons: Trauma.

    Overall, merchants have 3 levels in knowledge skills, 5 levels in mental skills, 2 levels in physical skills, and 2 levels in weapon skills.

    Merchants also receive an additional 6 skill points to spend.
