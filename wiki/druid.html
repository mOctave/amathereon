Druid

    Class Data
    Druid
    Wide Class Outlander
    Skill Points 4
    Specials Wildcraft, Druidcraft
    Languages Druic, Choose One
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 AGI, +1 CON, +1 WIS

    Druids understand the wilderness like nobody else. A skilled druid can even, with this knowledge, control the weather, and even an unskilled druid can be a great healer or hunter. Most druids live in isolation: if somebody seeks the help of a druid, they must first find them. However, druids do occasionally meet to discuss matters of importance.

    A druid is considered responsible for a patch of land, usually about 5 wapentake. They see to the health of the land and the people and animals who live on it. This is a druid's goal in life, to ensure that everything else can run smoothly. Few druids turn to Chaos, and those who do are usually executed by their own, although those who remain hidden can become exceptionally dangerous

    Wildcraft

    Druids receive a 50% bonus to any use of the Knowledge: Wilderness skill. Additionally, druids can choose a non-urban environment in which to gain a 20% bonus to tracking and stealth.

    Druidcraft

    Druids know much about the wilderness, and can use some basic magic related to the wilds.

    Select a Language

    Druids can choose one additional language in which they can both speak, read, and write. This language cannot be Thieves' Cant.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Outlanders gain a +1 bonus to agility and constitution.

    Druids gain an additional +1 bonus to wisdom.

    Skills

    Outlanders gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge: Medicine, Knowledge: Wilderness, Mental: Investigation, Mental: Perception, Physical, Physical: Endurance, and Weapons: Ranged.

    Druids gain an additional level in Knowledge: Arcana, Knowledge: History, Knowledge: Medicine, Mental: Investigation, and Physical: Sleight of Hand.

    Overall, druids have 5 levels in knowledge skills, 3 levels in mental skills, 3 levels in physical skills, and 1 level in weapon skills.

    Druids also receive an additional 4 skill points to spend.
