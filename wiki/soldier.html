Soldier

    Class Data
    Soldier
    Wide Class Warrior
    Skill Points 6
    Specials Veteran
    Languages None
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 STR, +2 RES

    Soldiers appear, at first glance, very ordinary and not especially skilled, but they tend to pick up many skills over the course of years of campaigning. Experienced soldiers can match even a knight in single combat, and they know both how and when to break the rules. Soldiers have been everywhere and seen everything: nothing fazes them easily, and they often have very surprising skill sets.

    Many distrust soldiers, because of their profession's bad reputation, but if you earn a soldier's trust they will not break it. For many, their word is all that is left to them after decades of killing, and they value it highly. For others, unfortunately, they no longer care who they harm: they will have their fun regardless of who it hurts.

    Veteran

    Soldiers gain weapon experience twice as fast.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Warriors gain a +1 bonus to strength and resilience.

    Soldiers gain an additional +1 bonus to resilience, for a total of +2.

    Skills

    Warriors gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge, Mental: Perception, Physical, Physical: Athletics, Physical: Intimidation, Weapons, and Weapons: Melee.

    Soldiers gain an additional level in Knowledge: Medicine, Knowledge: Wilderness, Physical: Endurance, Weapons: Melee, and Weapons: Slashing.

    Overall, soldiers have 3 levels in knowledge skills, 1 level in mental skills, 4 levels in physical skills, and 4 levels in weapon skills.

    Soldiers also receive an additional 6 skill points to spend.
