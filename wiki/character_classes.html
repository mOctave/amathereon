Character Classes

    In Amathereon, classes are divided into two levels: wide classes, and narrow classes. Your character's wide class represents where they've come from and what general kind of adventurer they are, whether they are a criminal, fighter, wanderer, magic user, or something that doesn't fit nicely into one of these categories. Narrow classes are much more specific: they have a much larger effect on your character's stats than its wide class does.

    There are six wide classes accessible by the player. One of them, the Citizen class, is used for characters that do not have any other class defined.
