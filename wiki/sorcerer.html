    Sorcerers are the most powerful of all magicians on Amathereon. Drawing power from Possibility, they can accomplish almost anything that could be, no matter how unlikely. Sorcerers that serve Chaos are especially powerful, as they can use its full power to their benefit.

    Unfortunately for sorcerers â€” and perhaps fortunately for everyone else, there's a catch. A sorcerer can only draw so much power from the plane of Possibility before their strength of will runs out and they start drawing strength from their own bodies. Many a sorcerer has accidentally killed themself trying to achieve greatness, not realizing how close they were to the edge before they crossed it.

    Overall, sorcerers are more cunning than they are wise. A sorcerer can make a complicated plan, but without the input of others it is likely to fall apart. Sorcerers who live long enough can become quite wise, but they still are often unaware of the limits of their own strength.

    Sorcery

    Sorcerers can use the plane of Possibility to cast powerful magics, while drawing power from their own body.

    Select a Languages

    Sorcerers can choose one additional language in which they can both speak, read, and write. This language cannot be Thieves' Cant or Druidic.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Mages gain a +1 bonus to intelligence and wisdom.

    Sorcerers gain an additional +1 bonus to intelligence, for a total of +2.

    Skills

    Mages gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge: Arcana, Knowledge: Linguistics, Knowledge: Medicine, Mental, Mental: Perception, Mental: Performance, and Physical: Sleight of Hand.

    Sorcerers gain an additional level in Mental: Intimidation, Mental: Investigation, Mental: Perception, Physical: Endurance, and Weapons: Slashing.

    Overall, sorcerers have 3 levels in knowledge skills, 6 levels in mental skills, 2 levels in physical skills, and 1 level in weapon skills.

    Sorcerers also receive an additional 5 skill points to spend.
