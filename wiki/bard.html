Bard

    Class Data
    Bard
    Wide Class Outlander
    Skill Points 4
    Specials Wildcraft, Musicianship
    Languages Choose One
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 AGI, +1 CON, +1 CHA

    Bards are wandering musicians, always in search of a song that truly describes the world as they see it. Bards also are generally skilled poets, and know much about the history of Amathereon. Many bards are almost aggressively charismatic, performing for anyone who will pay them, and mocking anyone who won't. Nonetheless, a bard that calls you a friend will treat you no diferently than any other close friend would.

    Very few people begin their lives wanting to be bards, but it is not a bad life. Although a bard often needs someone else to defend and protect them, they can make money almost anywhere. For this reason, many bards settle down after a while and forsake the life of a travelling performer, only to take it up again when they run out of gold once more.

    Wildcraft

    Bards receive a 50% bonus to any use of the Knowledge: Wilderness skill. Additionally, bards can choose a non-urban environment in which to gain a 20% bonus to tracking and stealth.

    Musicianship

    Bards can make music with a variety of instruments, and receive an additional 50% bonus to any use of the Mental: Performance skill.

    Select a Language

    Bards can choose one additional language in which they can both speak, read, and write. This language cannot be Thieves' Cant or Druidic.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Outlanders gain a +1 bonus to agility and constitution.

    Bards gain an additional +1 bonus to charisma.

    Skills

    Outlanders gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge: Medicine, Knowledge: Wilderness, Mental: Investigation, Mental: Perception, Physical, Physical: Endurance, and Weapons: Ranged.

    Bards gain an additional level in Knowledge: Culture, Knowledge: Linguistics, Mental: Deception, Mental: Performance, and Mental: Persuasion.

    Overall, bards have 4 levels in knowledge skills, 5 levels in mental skills, 2 levels in physical skills, and 1 level in weapon skills.

    Bards also receive an additional 4 skill points to spend.
