Barbarian

    Class Data
    Barbarian
    Wide Class Outlander
    Skill Points 4
    Specials Wildcraft, Calm Fury
    Languages Choose One
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 AGI, +1 STR, +1 CON

    "Barbarian" is perhaps too crude a name for an entire category of people. Many barbarians do not match the stereotypical barbarian at all, but have their own cultures and traditions that are no less valuable than anyone else's, if others bothered to learn about them. Barbarians tend to be strong fighters, and most deeply value their honour.

    The few barbarians that go to live in cities often end up lost, both physically and in their spirit. Because of the distance from their own culture and the disdain for them that is so obvious in those around them, they tend to forget their traditions, and often lend themselves a bad name. Nonetheless, some barbarians manage to accomplish great things, and many become fine warriors.

    Wildcraft

    Barbarians receive a 50% bonus to any use of the Knowledge: Wilderness skill. Additionally, barbarians can choose a non-urban environment in which to gain a 20% bonus to tracking and stealth.

    Calm Fury

    Barbarians gain a +1 bonus to hit for every percentage of their maximum HP they lose.

    Select a Language

    Barbarians can choose one additional language in which they can both speak, read, and write. This language cannot be Thieves' Cant or Druidic.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Outlanders gain a +1 bonus to agility and constitution.

    Barbarians gain an additional +1 bonus to strength.

    Skills

    Outlanders gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge: Medicine, Knowledge: Wilderness, Mental: Investigation, Mental: Perception, Physical, Physical: Endurance, and Weapons: Ranged.

    Barbarians gain an additional level in Knowledge, Physical: Athletics, Physical: Endurance, Weapons: Melee, and Weapons: Trauma.

    Overall, barbarians have 3 levels in knowledge skills, 2 levels in mental skills, 4 levels in physical skills, and 3 levels in weapon skills.

    Barbarians also receive an additional 4 skill points to spend.
