Cleric

    Class Data
    Cleric
    Wide Class Mage
    Skill Points 5
    Specials Divination
    Languages Choose One
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 INT, +1 WIS, +1 RES

    Clerics are the holiest of all mages. As such, they often despise others of their kind, especially necromancers who they often denounce, claiming that their customs are abominable. Interestingly, however, they are more closely related to necromancers than they are to any other kind of mage. Both use a connection with the plane of Entropy to call upon a higher power. For clerics, the power they call upon is that of their god, typically a Celestial. When their prayers are accepted, they can receive for a short while incredible power, without having to draw any strength from themselves to do so. When their prayers are ignored, they can achieve nothing but some minor healing.

    Divination

    Clerics can call upon their god to accomplish simple magic, along with some more complex elements.

    Select a Languages

    Clerics can choose one additional language in which they can both speak, read, and write. This language cannot be Thieves' Cant or Druidic.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Mages gain a +1 bonus to intelligence and wisdom.

    Clerics gain an additional +1 bonus to resilience.

    Skills

    Mages gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge: Arcana, Knowledge: Linguistics, Knowledge: Medicine, Mental, Mental: Perception, Mental: Performance, and Physical: Sleight of Hand.

    Clerics gain an additional level in Knowledge: Culture, Knowledge: Medicine, Mental, Mental: Intimidation, and Mental: Persuasion.

    Overall, clerics have 5 levels in knowledge skills, 6 levels in mental skills, 1 level in physical skills, and 0 levels in weapon skills.

    Clerics also receive an additional 5 skill points to spend.
