Assassin

    Class Data
    Assassin
    Wide Class Rogue
    Skill Points 4
    Specials Shadowy Connections, Kiling Blow
    Languages Thieves' Cant
    Ability Score Bonuses +2 DEX, +1 CHA

    Assassins are hated by almost all, yet they continue to find work all over Amathereon. Unlike thieves, assassins often manage to live long enough the amass quite a bit of wealth, but this is only because they can earn much more in a fixed amount of time if everything goes well. Assassins have nimble fingers and fast-working brains, and can be quite useful in a pinch.

    It is widely believed that assassins have no moral code. This is not true: an assassin will kill anybody, but only if there is some obvious benefit to them doing so. Because nobles have other ways of desposing of peasants, they only hire assassins to deal with someone of a similar level of wealth to them in most cases, and so assassins generally are tasked with killing potential employers.

    Shadowy Connections

    Assassins, like all Rogues, always have connections with the criminal underworld. As such, other people of low repute tend to trust them, and will give them information or even aid in exchange for enough gold.

    Killing Blow

    Assassins are proficient in killing people quickly, and receive double the critical chance of others.

    Thieves' Cant

    Assassins learn how to speak Thieves' Cant, and can usually write it phonetically using the Common character set.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Rogues gain a +1 bonus to dexterity and charisma.

    Assassins gain an additional +1 bonus to dexterity, for a total of +2.

    Skills

    Rogues gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge, Mental, Mental: Deception, Mental: Performance, Physical: Acrobatics, Weapons, and Weapons: Piercing.

    Assassins gain an additional level in Knowledge: Culture, Mental: Perception, Physical: Stealth, Weapons, and Weapons: Piercing.

    Overall, assassins have 2 levels in knowledge skills, 4 levels in mental skills, 2 levels in physical skills, and 4 levels in weapon skills.

    Assassins also receive an additional 4 skill points to spend.
