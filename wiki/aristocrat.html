Aristocrat

    Class Data
    Aristocrat
    Wide Class Specialist
    Skill Points 6
    Specials Interpersonal Skills
    Languages None
    Ability Score Bonuses +1 AGI, +1 CHA, +1 RES

    Everyone wants to trust an aristocrat, but few truly do. Most of the nobility are haughty and selfish wherever you go, but many are also shrewd politicans, skilled at manipulating people to do as they wish. Additionally, while aristocrats rarely have any marketable skills, they do have money, which is itself the means of gaining more. Many try to seek the patronage of a noble, and this gives them great power, to use as they will.

    Interpersonal Skills

    Aristocrats receive +20% effect to uses of Mental: Deception, Mental: Intimidation, Mental: Performance, and Mental: Persuasion.

    Ability Score Bonuses

    Aristocrats gain a +1 bonus to agility, charisma, and resilience.

    Skills

    Specialists gain a level in each of the following skills: Knowledge, Mental, Physical, and Weapons.

    Aristocrats gain an additional level in Knowledge: Culture, Knowledge: History, Knowledge: Linguistics, Mental: Intimdation, Mental: Persuasion, Physical: Acrobatics, Physical: Stealth and Weapons: Ranged.

    Overall, aristocrats have 4 levels in knowledge skills, 3 levels in mental skills, 3 levels in physical skills, and 2 levels in weapon skills.

    Aristocrats also receive an additional 6 skill points to spend.
